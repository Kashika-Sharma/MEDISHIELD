ℹ️ HOW THIS WORKS — REAL DARK WEB DETECTION
HIBP API (HaveIBeenPwned)
World's largest breach database. 14+ billion records. Used by governments & hospitals worldwide. When you enter an email, it checks against every known data breach in real-time.
Indian Context — DPDP Act 2023
India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 mandates hospitals to notify patients within 72 hours of a breach. MediShield automates this compliance workflow.
Risk Score Calculation
Score = (Breaches × 10) + (Health data types × 15 each) + (Non-health sensitive × 8 each) + Recency bonus (up to 8). Capped at 100. Critical = 70+, High = 45+, Medium = 20+.
What Data is Exposed?
Emails, passwords, medical records, Aadhaar-linked health IDs, insurance numbers — all trade on dark web forums. AIIMS breach (2022) exposed 40 lakh patient records.
